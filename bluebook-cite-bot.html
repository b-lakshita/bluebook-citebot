Bluebook Cite Bot

Checks and tidies the Bluebook (21st ed.) formatting of citations you've already drafted.

This tool only checks and suggests the correct Bluebook format for rough citations you've already prepared. It does not generate, look up, or invent citations, cases, or links on its own — if something is incomplete, it tells you what's missing rather than filling it in.
Formatting aid for citations you've already drafted, under The Bluebook (21st ed.). Does not generate or verify the substance of citations. Not legal advice.
